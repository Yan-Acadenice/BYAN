## ğŸ”¢ Complexity Scoring Algorithm

```
Total Score = InputScore + TypeScore + ContextScore + StepsScore + OutputScore

InputScore (0-20):
  - < 100 chars:     0
  - 100-500 chars:   5
  - 500-1000 chars:  10
  - > 1000 chars:    20

TypeScore (0-30):
  - simple:          0
  - format:          5
  - generate:        15
  - analysis:        30
  - reasoning:       30
  - creation:        30

ContextScore (0-20):
  - no context:      0
  - < 1000 chars:    5
  - 1000-5000:       10
  - > 5000:          20

StepsScore (0-15):
  - 1 step:          0
  - 2-3 steps:       8
  - > 3 steps:       15

OutputScore (0-15):
  - text:            0
  - json:            5
  - complex:         15
```
